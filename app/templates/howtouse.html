How To Use


To generate and image of either a dog, a math symbol, or a made up marvel superhero you first need to go to the page with you respective image. If you are trying to generate a dog or a math symbol you must input a valid dog species or math symbol into the input box(valid symbols/species are below and capital letters do not matter). Then to generate an image for any of the pages you need to click the generate button. This should generate images but might take a few seconds to load.
